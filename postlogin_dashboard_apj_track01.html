APJ – Discover Aruba Instant On Incentive program (“Program”)

PROGRAM PERIOD:

June 15, 2021 – April 30, 2022

WHO IS ELIGIBLE:

Authorized HPE resellers and those partners who do not have valid Agreements in place in EMEA who fulfill the below Eligibility Criteria ("Participants")

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:

Participants must meet the below criteria to be eligible for this Program:

  • Participants who have not sold either 3P or I5 Product Lines ("PLs") in the past

ELIGIBLE PRODUCTS:

The Eligible Products under the Program are 3P (wireless) and I5 PLs (wired) (“Eligible Products”).

AWARD:

The Winners under this Program will be entitled to a voucher for a free unit of Aruba Instant On AP11D through the sale of minimum $100 of Eligible Products and completion of Aruba Instant On Sales certification (“Award”). The maximum value of the cumulated Awards earned by a participant to ACTION Partner Club is limited to 2000 USD per quarter for partners who do not have valid Agreements in place and to 4000 USD per quarter for Aruba Business Partners.

WINNING CRITERIA:

The Participants will be rewarded with vouchers on the sell-through of either one unit of 3P or I5 as provided under the Eligible Products. The Winners will be rewarded only for those PLs that the Participants have not sold in the past.

Participants meeting the above winning criteria (“Winners”) shall be entitled to receive the Award.
